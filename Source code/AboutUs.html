Introduction

The Air Quality Index (AQI) system monitors air pollutants in Malaysia, providing a numerical value to represent air quality and its health impacts.

Objectives

  • Provide up-to-date air quality information.
  • Advise on health protection measures.
  • Allow users to choose their location and browse air quality statistics.
